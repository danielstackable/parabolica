BEng Mechanical Engineering

Nagoya University's Mechanical Engineering Program offers a comprehensive foundation in the core disciplines of mechanical, electromechanical, and aerospace engineering. Designed with flexibility in mind, the program allows students to explore a wide range of engineering topics—such as nano-scale technologies, energy systems, and transport innovation—before specializing in a chosen area. Early coursework focuses on mathematics and physics to build strong analytical skills, followed by project-based learning and hands-on lab experience from the third year onward. Students benefit from exposure to Japan’s globally renowned automotive industry through guest lectures, site visits, and partnerships with leading manufacturers. Located in the heart of Japan’s industrial capital, this program is ideal for students who want to work at the intersection of engineering innovation and the future of transportation. It forms part of Nagoya University’s broader Automotive Engineering track, which also includes Electrical and Information Engineering.
