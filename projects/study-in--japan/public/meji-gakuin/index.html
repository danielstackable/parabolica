Meiji Gakuin University

Meiji Gakuin University is a private liberal arts institution with campuses in Tokyo and Yokohama, offering English-medium bachelor’s programs in English Language & Culture and International Studies. The Tokyo campus is located in Shirokane, a quiet and leafy residential district in Minato City, just south of central Tokyo and near the Shibuya and Roppongi areas. Students benefit from a peaceful study environment while remaining well connected to Tokyo’s cultural and commercial hubs. The university emphasizes critical thinking, cross-cultural awareness, and global engagement—making it a popular choice for students pursuing international careers or further graduate study.

A touch of tradition, a world of insight—Meiji Gakuin connects Japan to the globe.
