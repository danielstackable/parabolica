Hiroshima University

Hiroshima University is a national university established in 1949, located in Higashihiroshima and Hiroshima City. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs with a strong focus on peace and international cooperation.

Study in a place of resilience and peace—Hiroshima University is committed to global harmony and academic excellence.
