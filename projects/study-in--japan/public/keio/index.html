Keio University

Keio University is Japan’s oldest private higher education institution, founded in 1858 in Tokyo. It is widely known for excellence in economics, business, and medicine.

Japan’s oldest private university and a symbol of innovation, Keio shapes tomorrow’s leaders in business, health, and society.
