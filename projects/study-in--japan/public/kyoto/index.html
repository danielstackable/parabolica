Kyoto University

Kyoto University is a leading national university in Kyoto, Japan, founded in 1897. It is internationally respected for research in chemistry, physics, and biology and has produced multiple Nobel laureates.

Excellence through inquiry—Kyoto University is one of Asia’s top research institutions in science and the humanities.
