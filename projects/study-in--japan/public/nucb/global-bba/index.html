Global BBA

The Global Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program at NUCB Undergraduate School offers a world-class business education entirely in English, designed for students seeking to develop the skills and mindset required for success in international business environments. Located at the scenic Nisshin Campus near Nagoya—a major hub for Japan’s automotive, aerospace, and robotics industries—the program provides a multicultural learning experience with direct relevance to global economic trends. The curriculum integrates core business disciplines including Management, Marketing, Finance, Accounting, Economics, and Entrepreneurship, alongside Liberal Arts to foster critical thinking and cross-cultural communication. Students gain a holistic understanding of global commerce, supported by small, discussion-based classes and a strong emphasis on case study learning. To support international integration, Japanese language courses are available at all levels. Graduates are prepared for careers in multinational corporations, startups, consulting, or further academic study in globally recognized business schools.
