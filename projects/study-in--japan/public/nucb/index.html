NUCB Undergraduate School

NUCB Undergraduate School is a private AACSB-accredited institution in Nisshin and downtown Nagoya, offering a fully English Global BBA program with case-method pedagogy and diverse international partnerships.

Be part of Japan's first English-taught Global BBA—case-based, global, and globally accredited.
