Eikei University of Hiroshima

Eikei University of Hiroshima is a public university founded by the Hiroshima Prefectural Government in 2021. Located in the globally recognized city of Hiroshima, EUH focuses on social system design, international studies, and sustainability-driven management. Its interdisciplinary curriculum is rooted in the UN SDGs and combines liberal arts with hands-on learning to train future global changemakers. With students from over 25 countries and partnerships across four continents, EUH offers a truly international, English-accessible environment built for the 22nd century.
