BA in Social System Design

The Bachelor of Arts in Social System Design at Eikei University of Hiroshima is a fully English-taught undergraduate program focused on addressing global challenges through interdisciplinary thinking and practical innovation. Grounded in the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), the program blends liberal arts education with applied training in areas such as public policy, data analysis, environmental studies, entrepreneurship, and international collaboration. Students learn to design solutions for complex social issues by working across disciplines and engaging in real-world projects with local communities and international partners. The curriculum includes mandatory internships, fieldwork, and overseas study opportunities, empowering graduates to become changemakers in public service, NGOs, global business, or graduate research. Located in Hiroshima—a symbol of peace and resilience—the program attracts students from over 25 countries, creating a diverse and globally oriented campus community.

Provider: Eikei University of Hiroshima

Eikei University of Hiroshima is a public university founded by the Hiroshima Prefectural Government in 2021. Located in the globally recognized city of Hiroshima, EUH focuses on social system design, international studies, and sustainability-driven management. Its interdisciplinary curriculum is rooted in the UN SDGs and combines liberal arts with hands-on learning to train future global changemakers. With students from over 25 countries and partnerships across four continents, EUH offers a truly international, English-accessible environment built for the 22nd century.
