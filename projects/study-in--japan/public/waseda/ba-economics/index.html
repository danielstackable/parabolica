BA in Economics

Waseda University's Bachelor of Arts in Economics provides a rigorous and versatile education in economic theory, quantitative analysis, and policy application. Students develop a solid foundation in microeconomics, macroeconomics, econometrics, and game theory, while also engaging with specialized courses that apply data-driven methods to real-world issues in development, finance, and public policy. The program emphasizes both theoretical depth and practical skills, supported by one of Asia’s largest experimental economics labs. Faculty members bring international research and policy expertise—including fieldwork across Asia and Africa, and leadership roles in Japan’s government advisory bodies. Graduates are well-prepared for impactful careers in government, finance, central banking, media, NGOs, international development, and academia.
