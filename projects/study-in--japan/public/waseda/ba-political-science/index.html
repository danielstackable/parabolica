BA in Political Science

Waseda University's Bachelor of Arts in Political Science offers a comprehensive, research-driven education in political thought, institutions, and global affairs. The program equips students with critical thinking and analytical skills to examine public issues, elections, policy-making, and international relations. Students explore core areas such as political philosophy, public opinion, international disputes, public policy, and comparative politics, while also gaining training in analytical and quantitative methods. Designed to foster leadership and civic responsibility, the degree prepares graduates to address complex political challenges in both domestic and global contexts. Taught by one of Japan’s leading political science faculties, the program encourages early specialization after a strong interdisciplinary foundation in the first two years.
