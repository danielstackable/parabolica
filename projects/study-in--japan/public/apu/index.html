Ritsumeikan Asia Pacific University (APU)

Ritsumeikan Asia Pacific University (APU) is a private institution in Beppu, Japan, founded in 2000. It is internationally focused, with courses taught in English and Japanese and a majority international student body.

At APU, the world comes to campus—study in English, live in Japan, and join a truly global student body.
