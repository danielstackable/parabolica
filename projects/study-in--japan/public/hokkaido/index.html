Hokkaido University

Hokkaido University is a leading national research university located in Sapporo, the vibrant northern city famous for its snow festivals, ramen, and Sapporo beer. Founded in 1876, it offers a broad range of English-taught bachelor programs and a dynamic research environment. The spacious, tree-lined campus combines historic charm with cutting-edge innovation, making it an ideal setting for globally minded students.

Study wide, think bold—Hokkaido University blends Arctic chill with global excellence.
