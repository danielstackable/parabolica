Tokyo Metropolitan University

Tokyo Metropolitan University is a public university founded in 1949. It offers a broad range of programs with a strong focus on science, urban policy, and environmental studies.

A leading urban university supporting Tokyo’s growth through cutting-edge science and policy education.
