International Christian University

International Christian University (ICU) is Japan’s first liberal arts college, located in Mitaka, a quiet western suburb of Tokyo about 30 minutes from Shinjuku. The university offers a fully bilingual education in English and Japanese, with a broad liberal arts curriculum spanning humanities, social sciences, and natural sciences. Set on a spacious, forested campus adjacent to Nogawa Park, ICU provides a peaceful study environment with easy access to central Tokyo. Ideal for globally minded students seeking both academic depth and a nature-rich setting.

ICU is where bilingual liberal arts and global vision meet.
